IRON MAN

Genius. Billionaire. Playboy. Philanthropist.

"I am Iron Man."

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is one of Marvel's most iconic superheroes. Portrayed by Robert Downey Jr., the character became the heart of the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

With unmatched intelligence and innovation, Tony Stark built the Iron Man suit to save lives and protect the world. His journey from a self-centered genius to a selfless hero inspired millions.

His ultimate sacrifice proved that heroes are defined not by their power, but by their choices. Iron Man will forever remain a symbol of courage, brilliance, and redemption.
